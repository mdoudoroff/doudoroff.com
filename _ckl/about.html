About the Cocktail Kingdom Library

The library is located in Cocktail Kingdom’s New York City Headquarters and is available for touring and research by appointment only. The extensive library hails from the private collection of Greg Boehm and is composed of more than 3000 books from the 1600’s to more modern day cocktail and spirit books, and ephemera.
