Kazuo Uyeda

1944–unknown

Japanese bartending master, author and inventor of the “hard shake”

Kazuo Uyeda is the proprietor of the Tender bar, in Ginza, which he opened in 1997. Uyeda has published several cocktail books, including “Cocktail Techniques”, which has been translated and republished outside Japan, exporting elements of Japanese cocktail culture. Uyeda is particularly well known for a stylized cocktail shaking technique he calls the “hard shake”.
