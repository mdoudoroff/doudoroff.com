Real Dutch Genever

Genever—real genever—is a delicious [malty] multi-grain distillate (historically lots of rye) with masterful hints of flavour from a small amount of juniper and (usually a small number of) other botanicals. Think of it as unaged whisky's complex daddy. Learn more
