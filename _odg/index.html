Old Duff Genever is not gin, and not “botanical whiskey”. Old Duff Genever is genever, the spirit that had already conquered the world well before Shakespeare, the Mona Lisa, steam power, and electricity came on the scene.

Old Duff Genever is Real: authentic, honest, and transparent.

Old Duff Genever is Dutch: handcrafted in Holland, using a windmill from 1785 and the seventh generation of a distilling family dating to 1777. Other countries can make genever. Holland defines it.

Every drop in each bottle of Old Duff Genever 100% Maltwine and Old Duff Genever 40% ABV was milled, mashed, fermented, distilled and bottled in Schiedam, Holland.
