The Martini Expo is a first-of-its-kind convention devoted to the greatest cocktail of all time. The history, art and culture of the Martini, as well as the components that comprise it, will be celebrated through a full day of seminars, tastings, presentations, guest bartender appearances, book signings, food, and more, including a Martini dinner held the evening before at Confidant, and an after party at a location to be announced. Don’t miss the cocktail event of the season.
