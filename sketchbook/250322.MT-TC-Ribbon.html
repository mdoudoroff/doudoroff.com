Major Tom/Tetrachords/Ribbon/Data Bender sketch

Impetus: try out a perma-patched arrangement with the ribbon controller, continue to validate Major Tom/Tetrachords combo, simple demonstration of Data Bender for adding complementary harmonic parts with minimum of effort.

Two parts (E370) driven by Major Tom and Tetrachords, each with ADSR and Atlas filtering. Doepfer ribbon controller used to manually animate the wavetables during recording.

Both parts separately sent to Data Bender, which adds repeats at various clock divisions modulated by sample and hold.

Basic percussion from A8 driven by remaining two channels of Major Tom, heavily filtered in Bitwig (with some filter modulation there). Minimal mixing and some Valhalla Room.
