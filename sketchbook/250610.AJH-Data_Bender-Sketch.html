Two parts run through the whole sketch, each AJH voices, recorded first, simultaneously: one is a plodding bass line; the other is a higher melodic part. The bass line is simply clocked notes and pitches using an LFO as the pitch voltage source run through a quantizer. The higher part is also clocked, but I am pitching it by hand from a Keystep and requantizing it in the quantizer. Most essentially, this second part runs through a clocked Data Bender, which hacks up and rearranges and repeats what would otherwise be a dreadfully rudimentary melody. No sequencers are employed in this sketch.

To these two parts, I later added some percussion and other sounds as overdubs, and did some editing and plugin effects work in Bitwig. Valhalla Room is providing reverb in various ways, and Transit 2 is used to manipulate the bass line at various points.
